Puppy Collector Game

Instructions:

    1. Take a look at the randomly generated Target Number, your goal is to match that number.
    2. Your Tally will increase each time your click on a puppy.
    3. Each puppy will have a random number associated with it, and that's what is added to your tally.
    4. Careful though, if you go over the Target Number, you get a Loss, if you match, you earn a Win.
